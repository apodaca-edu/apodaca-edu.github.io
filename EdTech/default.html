About Me

I am a 7th year educator endorsed in secondary math and science. I teach in southern Colorado.  I was born and raised in Colorado.  My background is in forestry, but my passion for learning led me into education.

My educational philosophy is that every person is entitled to a quality education.  I live and work in an rural district where need for quality education is more important now than ever.  We have come a long way in the last few years, but we have quite a bit of work ahead of us to help students become thinkers, achievers, and leaders.

I am pursuing a degree in educational technology for three main reasons.  First, I believe that technology can improve and help deliver quality education, especially in rural areas.  I want to teach individuals how to use technology to become more efficient and effective.  And finally, educational technology is the next logical career choice for me based on my personal philosophy and interests.
